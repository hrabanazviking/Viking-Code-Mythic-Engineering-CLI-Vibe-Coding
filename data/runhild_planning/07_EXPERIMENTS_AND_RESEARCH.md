# Experiments and Research Agenda

## Planning Research
- Compare beam search vs graph planning for quest orchestration.
- Evaluate retrieval-augmented planning with confidence gating.
- Test chain-of-thought suppression vs explicit rationale logs.

## Memory Research
- Benchmark chunk sizing strategies.
- Compare dense-only vs hybrid retrieval.
- Test recency weighting vs semantic salience weighting.

## Narrative Research
- Measure perceived coherence under different context budgets.
- Evaluate NPC believability with trait drift enabled.
- Test dynamic pacing against static pacing.

## Reliability Research
- Chaos testing for dispatcher and memory backends.
- Error budget policy simulation.
- Auto-remediation threshold tuning.
