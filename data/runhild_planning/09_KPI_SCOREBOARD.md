# KPI Scoreboard

## Engineering KPIs
- Mean time to detect (MTTD)
- Mean time to recover (MTTR)
- Change failure rate
- Defect escape rate
- Test flake rate

## Product/Narrative KPIs
- Session coherence score
- Quest completion rate
- Player return rate
- Player satisfaction signal
- NPC believability rating

## Efficiency KPIs
- Token cost per session
- Planning latency
- Retrieval hit quality
- Context waste ratio

## Governance KPIs
- Lore contradiction rate
- Audit coverage
- Policy violation rate
